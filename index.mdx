## How it works

<Steps>
  <Step title="Fork and clone">
    Fork [ArnasDon/wacrm](https://github.com/ArnasDon/wacrm) on GitHub, then clone your fork locally.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Configure credentials">
    Copy `.env.local.example` to `.env.local` and fill in your Supabase URL, anon key, service role key, encryption key, and Meta app secret.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Run migrations">
    Apply the 30 SQL migration files in `supabase/migrations/` against your Supabase project to create the full schema.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Connect WhatsApp">
    Register your Meta Cloud API phone number, point the webhook at `/api/whatsapp/webhook`, and paste your token into the Settings page.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Deploy">
    Push to Hostinger (recommended), Vercel, Railway, or any Node.js host. The app runs on standard `next start`.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Note>
  Wacrm is a **template**, not a managed service. You own the code, the database, and the data. Forking is the expected flow — customise freely.
</Note>
